Challenge

A Fortune 500 manufacturer with 50+ facilities worldwide struggled with disparate ERP systems, manual consolidation taking 15 days monthly, and no real-time visibility into global operations.

Solution

Implemented unified integration platform connecting SAP, Oracle, and legacy systems. Automated inter-company eliminations, currency conversions, and consolidation. Deployed real-time dashboards for executive team.

Results

  • Month-end close reduced from 15 to 3 days
  • $2.5M annual cost savings
  • 100% accuracy in inter-company reconciliations
  • Real-time P&L visibility across all entities
