Payment & Refund Policy

Clear Terms for All Transactions

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all eligible services. If you're not completely satisfied with our services, you may request a full refund within 14 days of purchase.

1. Payment Terms

1.1 Accepted Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
  • Debit Cards
  • ACH Bank Transfers
  • Wire Transfers (for amounts over $10,000)
  • Corporate Checks (subject to approval)
  • PayPal Business Payments

1.2 Currency

All prices are listed in United States Dollars (USD) unless otherwise specified. For international clients, currency conversion rates applied by your payment provider may apply.

1.3 Payment Schedule

  • One-Time Services: Payment due upon order confirmation
  • Subscription Services: Billed monthly or annually in advance
  • Custom Projects: 50% deposit upfront, 50% upon completion (negotiable for large projects)
  • Enterprise Contracts: Net 30 payment terms available (subject to credit approval)

1.4 Taxes

Prices do not include applicable taxes. You are responsible for all applicable federal, state, and local taxes, duties, or other governmental assessments. We will add applicable taxes to your invoice where required by law.

2. Billing Information

2.1 Accuracy of Information

You agree to provide current, complete, and accurate purchase and account information for all purchases. You agree to promptly update your account information, including email address, payment method, and payment card expiration date.

2.2 Billing Disputes

If you believe there is an error on your bill, you must contact us within 30 days of the invoice date. We will investigate the dispute and respond within 10 business days.

2.3 Late Payments

Invoices not paid within the specified terms are subject to:

  • Late fee of 1.5% per month or the maximum permitted by law
  • Suspension of services after 30 days past due
  • Termination of services after 60 days past due
  • Collection proceedings for amounts over 90 days past due

3. Refund Policy

3.1 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We stand behind the quality of our services with a 14-day money-back guarantee:

  • Applies to all new service purchases
  • Request must be submitted within 14 calendar days of purchase
  • Full refund of the purchase price
  • No questions asked for standard services
  • Custom work may require explanation for quality improvement purposes

3.2 Eligible Services for 14-Day Guarantee

  • Software licenses and subscriptions
  • Standard consulting packages
  • Training and certification programs
  • Support and maintenance plans
  • Cloud service subscriptions

3.3 Services Not Eligible for 14-Day Guarantee

  • Custom development work after code delivery
  • Third-party licenses and products
  • Domain name registrations
  • Services already consumed or deployed
  • Enterprise contracts with negotiated terms

3.4 How to Request a Refund

To request a refund within the 14-day guarantee period:

  1. Contact our support team at support@gmeltdus.com
  2. Include your order number and purchase date
  3. Briefly explain the reason for your refund request (optional)
  4. We will process your request within 2 business days
  5. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 5-10 business days

3.5 Refunds After 14 Days

After the 14-day guarantee period, refunds may be considered on a case-by-case basis for:

  • Service outages exceeding SLA guarantees
  • Failure to deliver promised services
  • Billing errors or duplicate charges
  • Documented service deficiencies

3.6 Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued for:

  • Unused portions of prepaid services (pro-rated)
  • Service credits for performance issues
  • Negotiated settlements for disputed services

4. Subscription Services

4.1 Automatic Renewal

Subscription services automatically renew at the end of each billing period unless cancelled. You will be notified 7 days before renewal with the renewal date and amount.

4.2 Cancellation

  • Monthly subscriptions: Cancel anytime, effective at the end of the current billing month
  • Annual subscriptions: Cancel anytime, effective at the end of the current billing year
  • No refunds for partial periods unless within the 14-day guarantee

4.3 Price Changes

We reserve the right to modify subscription prices with 30 days' notice. You may cancel your subscription before the price change takes effect.

5. Custom Projects and Enterprise Services

5.1 Project Deposits

  • Standard projects: 50% deposit required to begin work
  • Large projects (over $50,000): Payment milestones negotiated individually
  • Deposits are non-refundable after work has commenced

5.2 Change Orders

Changes to project scope after work has begun will require:

  • Written approval of scope changes
  • Adjustment to project timeline
  • Additional payment for expanded scope

5.3 Project Cancellation

If you cancel a custom project:

  • Before work begins: Full refund of deposit
  • After work begins: Payment for work completed plus 10% cancellation fee
  • After 50% completion: Full project fee is due

6. Service Credits

6.1 SLA Credits

Service credits may be issued for failure to meet Service Level Agreements:

  • Calculated based on downtime and affected services
  • Applied to your next invoice
  • Cannot exceed monthly service fees
  • Not redeemable for cash

6.2 Promotional Credits

Promotional credits:

  • Have expiration dates specified at issuance
  • Cannot be combined unless specified
  • Have no cash value
  • Are non-transferable

7. Chargebacks and Disputes

7.1 Chargeback Policy

Before initiating a chargeback with your credit card company:

  • Contact us first to resolve the issue
  • Allow 5 business days for resolution
  • Provide documentation of the issue

7.2 Fraudulent Chargebacks

Fraudulent chargebacks may result in:

  • Immediate account suspension
  • Collection proceedings
  • Reporting to credit agencies
  • Legal action for recovery of funds and costs

8. Security and Fraud Prevention

8.1 Payment Security

We employ industry-standard security measures:

  • PCI DSS compliance for card processing
  • SSL encryption for all transactions
  • Tokenization of payment information
  • Regular security audits

8.2 Fraud Detection

We reserve the right to:

  • Verify large or unusual transactions
  • Request additional identification
  • Decline suspicious transactions
  • Report fraudulent activity to authorities

9. International Payments

9.1 Currency Conversion

For international payments:

  • Prices displayed in USD
  • Conversion rates determined by payment processor
  • Customer responsible for conversion fees

9.2 International Taxes and Duties

International customers are responsible for:

  • VAT, GST, or other applicable taxes
  • Import duties or customs fees
  • Any withholding taxes

10. Modifications to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Payment & Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to our website. Your continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the modified policy.
